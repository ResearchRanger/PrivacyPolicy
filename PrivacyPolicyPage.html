Privacy Policy for TappyTalker Easy Reads

We take privacy, security, and safety very seriously. We strive to provide an environment conducive to education and enjoyment for your child. Our goal is to provide an effective learning space as safe as a "Sandbox".

The Personal Information we Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

The Data we Collect

We do not collect any data.

The Data we Share

The app requests the Google Play Client to perform a license verification check. The Google Play Client uses your Google ID to verify that the app has originated (been purchased) from the Google Play store. Otherwise, the app does not share any data.

The Permissions we Request

We use only three app permissions:

System Alert Window

We may use this to advise you of an error condition using a temporary message window.

Check License

We use this, for security purposes, to verify that the TappyTalker Easy Reads app has originated (been purchased) from the Google Play online store. Currently the Google Play store is the only store approved to sell the app.

Internet

The app will only access the internet to perform the "Check License" operation (above). Otherwise, there is no direct way to use or access the internet from within the app.

Advertising

The app does not contain advertisements.
